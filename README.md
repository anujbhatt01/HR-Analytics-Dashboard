# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard: Employee Attrition Analysis

## 🔍 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing employee data to understand **attrition patterns and workforce trends** using **Python (EDA)** and **Power BI Dashboard**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze employee attrition behavior
* Identify key factors affecting employee turnover
* Build an interactive HR dashboard
* Provide actionable business insights

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
* Power BI
* CSV Dataset

---

## 📊 Key Insights

### 🔹 Attrition Overview

* Total Employees: **1470**
* Attrition Count: **237**
* Attrition Rate: **16.1%**

---

### 🔹 Salary vs Attrition

* Employees with salary **below 5K show highest attrition (163 cases)**
* Higher salary groups show significantly lower attrition

👉 **Insight:** Salary is a major factor influencing attrition

---

### 🔹 Age Group Analysis

* Highest attrition in **26–35 age group (116 employees)**

👉 **Insight:** Mid-career professionals are more likely to leave

---

### 🔹 Job Role Analysis

* Laboratory Technician, Sales Executive, and Research Scientist show highest attrition

👉 **Insight:** Technical and sales roles have higher turnover

---

### 🔹 Gender Distribution

* Male attrition: **140**
* Female attrition: **79**

👉 **Insight:** Attrition is higher among male employees

---

### 🔹 Education Field

* Life Sciences (38%) and Medical (27%) show higher attrition

---

### 🔹 Years at Company

* Higher attrition observed in early years

👉 **Insight:** Employee retention strategies should focus on early tenure
